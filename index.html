Nurturing The Future Generation

Since 2017, Validus Circle (VC) is a visionary youth-led organization based in Lilongwe, Malawi, led by young women. VC advocates for economic empowerment for the youth and children by using Health ( SRHR, HIV, Mental Health), Education, Gender Equality, Civic Engagement, and Advocacy.
